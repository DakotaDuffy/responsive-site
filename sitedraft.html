Who am I?

My name is Dakota Duffy. I'm a 21-year-old game developer, writer, programmer, college student and maker living in beautiful Southern California.

You can typically find me struggling to choose a book to read/watching basketball/hastily developing for the One Hour Game Jam/managing an NBA fantasy league/writing a blog post/collecting hats/listening to 90's hip hop/drinking horchata/creating an interactive experience
